# Growth Flow

Orchestrates Phase 5 using growth-strategist.

## Prerequisites
- Completed Phase 4 (5+ paying customers, repeatable outreach)
- Product-market fit signals: retention, referrals, easier conversations
- If no revenue, route back to monetize-flow

## The Shift: Product-Market Fit → Product-Channel Fit

Once monetization is working, everything changes. It's time to figure out which distribution channel or sales and marketing method is going to be most effective specifically for YOUR product. This is product-channel fit.

## Sequence

```
1. Verify PMF signals (retention rate, referral rate, churn)
2. growth-strategist: Growth Trifecta plan
   -> Outreach at scale blueprint
   -> 4-week content calendar
   -> Retargeting strategy with budget
   -> process-guardian reviews
3. growth-strategist: Automation roadmap
   -> Prioritized by time saved
   -> process-guardian reviews
4. User executes growth plan channel by channel
```

## The Growth Trifecta

Build these in sequence, not simultaneously:

### Channel 1: Personalized Outreach at Scale (Month 1)
- Systematize what's already working
- Increase volume 2-3x with automation
- This is the foundation. Never stop.

### Channel 2: Organic Content (Month 2-3)
- Start posting on the platform where customers spend time
- LinkedIn for B2B, Twitter/X for developer/tech, Instagram/TikTok for consumer
- Share: customer wins, behind-the-scenes, lessons learned, hot takes
- Track engagers. These become outreach targets.

### Channel 3: Paid Retargeting (Month 3-4)
- Install tracking pixels on website
- Build custom audiences from organic engagers + site visitors
- Retarget on Meta, Google, YouTube
- Start budget: $500-1000/month on retargeting only
- Warm audiences convert 3-5x better than cold

## The LinkedIn Engager Flywheel

This is where organic content and outreach combine powerfully:
1. Post content on LinkedIn about your topic
2. People comment and react (showing interest)
3. Extract engagers using a prospecting tool (Bond, Phantombuster, or similar)
4. Qualify: filter for ICP matches
5. Research each person, then do personalized outreach
6. Use `scripts/linkedin_engagers.py` to generate outreach plan and templates

## Automation Priority Order

Only automate things that work manually first. Over-automating is a dramatic mistake that wastes time on things that don't move the needle. The goal is to scale your company without scaling headcount.

Automate in this order:
1. CRM follow-up sequences (AI agents for revenue ops — OpenAI Agents, Claude Code, or similar)
2. Content scheduling (batch create, auto-publish)
3. Prospect research (AI-assisted enrichment)
4. Customer onboarding (automated welcome flow)
5. Reporting dashboards

## Phase 5 Checklist (Ongoing)

- [ ] Outreach systematized at 2-3x previous volume
- [ ] Organic content posting 3-5x per week
- [ ] Retargeting campaigns live with positive ROAS
- [ ] At least one process automated and saving 5+ hours/week
- [ ] Revenue growing month over month
- [ ] Customer acquisition cost (CAC) measured and improving

## Common Mistakes to Flag
- Trying all 3 channels at once (do them in sequence)
- Stopping outreach once content starts working
- Over-automating before processes are proven
- Hiring people before automating with AI
- Spending on cold ads before retargeting warm audiences
- Not measuring CAC and LTV

## Failure Modes
- **Organic content doesn't gain traction after 4 weeks:** Test a different platform or content format. Not every platform works for every product.
- **Retargeting ads have poor ROAS:** Your audience might be too small. Build organic first, retarget later.
- **Growth stalls:** Go back to customer conversations. The answer is always with customers.

## Quality Gates
- [ ] PMF verified before growth planning starts
- [ ] Outreach systematized at 2-3x previous volume
- [ ] Organic content posting 3-5x per week
- [ ] Retargeting campaigns live with measurable ROAS
- [ ] At least one process automated, saving 5+ hours/week

## This Phase Never Ends
Growth is iterative. Keep adding channels, keep automating, keep talking to customers.
